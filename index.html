About Me


Hi! I'm Levi Stenton. I'm a recent games development graduate from SAE Qantm, majoring in design and specializing in writing.


I'm passionate about building worlds for people to explore and writing the stories behind them. My goals are always to create and emotionally evocative experience for people to enjoy.


I'm an Australian developer living in Brisbane and I've had the pleasure of working on a variety of different projects over the years, which you can see for yourself above.

Good Morning

Good Morning is a feeling-based game centred around the idea of home. I worked on this game as the vision holder and one of the level designers for my Studio 2 module during my studies. The core idea of the narrative (carrying out your morning routine) was entirely my responsibility, along with designing and creating around it.

Tempus

Tempus is a story-based walking sim focussed around the core idea of making a game entirely comprised within one location. I worked on this game as vision holder and the lead level designer for my Studio 1 module during my studies. I designed the narrative progression and wrote and directed the script for this game, being entirely responsible for idea of the core loop and the intended experience.
